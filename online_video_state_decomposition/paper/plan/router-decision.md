# Router Decision

Route to **research-latex-writing empirical workflow**.

## Rationale

The main title claim requires new hidden-state probes, trained path ablations, matched-budget downstream evaluation, and measured latency. The literature map and existing local experiments constrain the hypothesis, but citations alone cannot establish the proposed state decomposition.

## Alternative Path

Route to `arxiv-paper-writer` only if the project abandons the method claim and becomes a survey or benchmark synthesis of structured attention and streaming memory. That is not the current objective.

## Adversarial Challenge

The strongest argument against the empirical route is that online robust PCA, CausalMem, StateKV, Flash-VStream, and event-centric memories already contain most primitives, leaving only an engineering combination. This objection holds unless the probes demonstrate mechanism-specific complementarity and the system improves a matched fixed-memory/fixed-compute Pareto frontier with real tail-latency gains.

## Final Verdict

Remain empirical, but keep novelty provisional. The paper may proceed to full prose only after the three probe gates are resolved and the contribution map is narrowed to the components that survive.
